Meet our founder, Ambrose Eterna:

As the visionary founder of EternaFunplex, Ambrose Eterna dedicated his life to creating unforgettable entertainment experiences for visitors of all ages. With a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, Ambrose transformed EternaFunplex into Pineview's premier entertainment destination. Sadly, Ambrose passed away in 1999, but his legacy lives on through the megacomplex, which continues to thrive as a testament to his boundless creativity and entrepreneurial spirit. Join us in honoring Ambrose's memory and celebrating his enduring legacy at EternaFunplex today!
