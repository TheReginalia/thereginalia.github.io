Welcome to Eterna Funplex!

At EternaFunplex, we're dedicated to providing unforgettable entertainment experiences for visitors of all ages. As Celestria Haven's premier entertainment destination, we offer a diverse array of attractions and amenities designed to spark joy, foster creativity, and create lasting memories for our guests.

Our Story

Founded in 1989 by visionary entrepreneur Mr. Ambrose Eterna, EternaFunplex has been a beloved fixture in the community for three decades. From our humble beginnings as a small arcade and pizza parlor to our current status as a sprawling megacomplex, we've remained committed to our mission of delivering fun-filled adventures in a safe and welcoming environment.

Our Commitment to Safety
