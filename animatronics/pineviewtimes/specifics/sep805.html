Nebula Ninja Nyx Mysteriously Vanishes from EternaFunplex

The disappearance of the Nebula Ninja Nyx animatronic has left visitors to the EternaFunplex baffled and unnerved. Reports of shadowy figures and ghostly sightings have circulated, fueling speculation about the fate of the elusive animatronic. The incident has added to the complex's reputation as a place of mystery and danger, with visitors wary of what other secrets lie hidden within its walls.
