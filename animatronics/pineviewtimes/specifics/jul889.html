EternaFunplex Grand Opening Wows Pineview Residents

Pineview residents were treated to a spectacle of entertainment as the EternaFunplex celebrated its grand opening. The megacomplex, touted as a haven of fun and excitement, welcomed visitors with open arms, offering a plethora of attractions ranging from arcades to food courts. The event marked the beginning of a new era in entertainment for the town, promising endless hours of enjoyment for visitors of all ages.

Article Date: July 8, 1989
