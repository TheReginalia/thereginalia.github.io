Pineview High School Wins State Championship in Robotics Competition

Pineview High School's robotics team has emerged victorious in the state championship, showcasing their engineering prowess and teamwork. The team, known for their innovative designs and strategic thinking, defeated competitors from across the state to claim the title. Their success reflects the town's dedication to STEM education and provides inspiration for future generations of aspiring engineers and technologists.

Article Date: March 9, 2020
