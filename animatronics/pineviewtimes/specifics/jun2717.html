Local Community Garden Wins Award for Sustainable Practices

The Pineview Community Garden has received recognition for its sustainable practices and dedication to environmental stewardship. The garden, which provides fresh produce to residents and promotes green living, was honored with an award from the state's Department of Environmental Conservation. The accolade is a testament to the hard work and dedication of the garden's volunteers and underscores Pineview's commitment to sustainability.

Article Date: June 27, 2017
