Pianist Melody Malfunction Sparks Haunting Experience for Guests

Guests at the EternaFunplex were left shaken after a malfunction in the Pianist Melody animatronic turned a grand reopening event into a haunting experience. The animatronic's melodies twisted into discordant cacophony, accompanied by an eerie blue glow that sent chills down the spines of onlookers. The incident has sparked rumors of supernatural phenomena at the complex, leaving visitors wary of its dark secrets.

Article Date: July 21, 1995
