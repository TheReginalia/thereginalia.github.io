Pineview Welcomes New Mayor in Inauguration Ceremony

Pineview ushered in the new year with the inauguration of its new mayor, Thomas Richardson. In a ceremonial event attended by residents and local officials, Mayor Richardson outlined his vision for the town's future, emphasizing initiatives to promote economic growth, community development, and environmental sustainability. The inauguration marks the beginning of a new chapter for Pineview under Mayor Richardson's leadership.

Article Date: January 1, 2019
