Maintenance Crew Disappears Without a Trace at EternaFunplex

Mystery shrouds the EternaFunplex as a maintenance crew vanishes without a trace during routine repairs on the animatronics. Despite extensive searches, no sign of the missing workers has been found, leaving authorities baffled and visitors uneasy. The incident has cast a shadow over the megacomplex, fueling speculation about foul play and ghostly encounters within its walls.

Article Date: October 3, 1998
