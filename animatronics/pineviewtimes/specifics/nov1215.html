Local Charity Gala Raises Funds for Homeless Shelter

Pineview's annual charity gala was a resounding success, raising significant funds for the town's homeless shelter. Attendees enjoyed an evening of fine dining, live music, and silent auctions, all in support of a worthy cause. The event highlights the generosity and compassion of Pineview's residents, who came together to make a positive impact on the community.

Article Date: November 12, 2015
