EternaFunplex Condemned Amidst Safety Concerns

The once-thriving EternaFunplex has been condemned by city authorities following a series of safety incidents and reports of missing persons. The decision to close the megacomplex comes amid mounting concerns about the safety of its attractions and the welfare of its visitors. While the closure marks the end of an era for Pineview', it also serves as a stark reminder of the dangers lurking within the abandoned complex, leaving residents to wonder what mysteries lie hidden within its walls.

Article Date: December 20, 2018
