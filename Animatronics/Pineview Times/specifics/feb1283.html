Local Cow Declares Intent to Pursue Career in Stand-Up Comedy

Pineview's bovine population is abuzz with excitement as Daisy the Cow announces her plans to pursue a career in stand-up comedy. Known for her quick wit and impeccable timing, Daisy has been entertaining fellow farm animals and farmers alike with her humorous anecdotes and moo-ving punchlines. With dreams of headlining her own comedy special, Daisy's comedic aspirations have brought laughter and joy to the entire community, proving that even cows have a sense of humor.

Article Date: Febuary 12 1983
