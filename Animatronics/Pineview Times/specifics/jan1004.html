Pineview Welcomes First Female Mayor in Town's History

Pineview made history with the election of its first female mayor, Sarah Thompson, in a landmark victory for gender equality and representation in local government. Mayor Thompson, a longtime community advocate and former city council member, pledged to bring fresh ideas and inclusive leadership to Pineview, promising to work tirelessly for the betterment of all residents.

Article Date: January 10, 2004
