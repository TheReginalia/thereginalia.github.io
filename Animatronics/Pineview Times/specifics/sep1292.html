Malfunction at EternaFunplex Causes Widespread Panic

Chaos ensued at the EternaFunplex as a malfunction in the Sparky the Electric Jester animatronic led to a widespread power outage and panic among visitors. Witnesses reported screams and confusion as darkness enveloped the complex, prompting urgent calls for assistance. While no injuries were reported, the incident raised questions about safety protocols at the popular entertainment venue.

Article Date: September 12, 1992
