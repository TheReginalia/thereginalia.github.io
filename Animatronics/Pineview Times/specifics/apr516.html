Local Chicken Crosses Road, Answers Age-Old Question

In a momentous event that has sparked philosophical debate across Pineview, a local chicken has finally crossed the road, putting an end to the age-old question of why poultry ventures to the other side. Witnesses were astonished as the plucky bird confidently navigated traffic with a sense of purpose, leaving onlookers to ponder the deeper meaning behind its journey. The event has left residents scratching their heads and contemplating the mysteries of life, liberty, and the pursuit of crossing roads.

Article Date: April 15 2016
