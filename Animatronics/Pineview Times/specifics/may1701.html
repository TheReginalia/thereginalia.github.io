Bio the Tinkerer Rampage Causes Chaos at EternaFunplex

Panic erupted at the EternaFunplex when the Bio the Tinkerer animatronic went on a rampage after being taunted by a guest. Mechanical traps and chaos ensued as the animatronic unleashed its fury, leaving visitors fleeing for safety. The incident has raised concerns about the safety of the animatronics and the need for increased security measures at the megacomplex.

Article Date: May 17, 2001
