Welcome to the SCP Foundation

Welcome, brave explorer, to the SCP Foundation. You have entered a world shrouded in mystery, where the extraordinary becomes reality and the impossible is our everyday.

The SCP Foundation is a global organization dedicated to the containment and study of anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena. Our mission is simple yet critical: Secure, Contain, Protect. We strive to shield humanity from the dangers lurking just beyond the veil of normalcy, ensuring that the unknown remains unknown.

Join us as we delve into the unknown, standing vigilant against the shadows that seek to breach the walls of reality. Your journey with the SCP Foundation begins here, where secrecy and science collide to safeguard humanity's future.

Secure. Contain. Protect.
