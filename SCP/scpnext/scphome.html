Welcome to the SCP Foundation

The SCP Foundation is a secret organization dedicated to securing, containing, and protecting anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena. Our mission is to ensure that these anomalies are kept out of the hands of the public, preventing widespread panic and maintaining global normalcy. Our personnel are trained to handle and study these entities, ensuring that they are safely contained and that any potential threats are mitigated.

Secure. Contain. Protect.
